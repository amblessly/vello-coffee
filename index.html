Our Story

Rooted in
Craftsmanship

Vello & Co. began in a small warehouse on the edge of the city — a roaster's obsession with the bean beneath the brew. We travel twice a year to visit the farms whose names appear on our bags: the hillside growers of Yirgacheffe, the micro-lots of Huila, the shade-grown plots of Sumatra.

Each variety is roasted in small batches, cupped daily, and served within days of roasting. We believe coffee is a living thing — and we honour that.
