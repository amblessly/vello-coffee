# ☕ Vello & Co.

A modern coffee shop website designed to showcase a premium café experience through elegant visuals, handcrafted storytelling, and a fully responsive user interface.

Inspired by artisan coffee houses, Vello & Co. combines warm branding, detailed menu presentation, featured specialties, team introductions, and reservation functionality into a polished web experience.

## Overview

Vello & Co. is a fictional specialty coffee brand created as a front-end web development project. The goal was to design a website that feels inviting, premium, and authentic while maintaining a clean user experience across desktop and mobile devices.

The website highlights the café's story, handcrafted menu, signature drinks, baristas, and reservation system through a modern and visually engaging layout.

## Features

* Responsive design for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
* Hero section with strong brand identity
* Café story and company background
* Interactive menu showcase
* Signature drinks section
* Featured barista profiles
* Reservation form
* Smooth scrolling navigation
* Modern coffee-inspired design system

## Sections

### Hero

A welcoming introduction to the café featuring branding, call-to-action buttons, and visual storytelling.

### Our Story

Shares the fictional journey of Vello & Co., focusing on craftsmanship, sourcing, and specialty coffee culture.

### Menu

Displays the café's core coffee offerings with descriptions and pricing.

### House Specials

Highlights signature drinks unique to the café.

### Baristas

Introduces the people behind the coffee and adds personality to the brand.

### Reserve a Seat

Allows visitors to book a table through a clean reservation form.

### Footer

Contains business information, operating hours, social links, and branding.
